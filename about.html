About Us

Mr. Sankaram, Founder

Spinfra Constructions was established in January 2003 by team of qualified and experienced Civil engineers from the industry. It has engineered a targeted offering of quality beyond expectation. Through collaborations with the foremost talents in the field, the usage of the finest materials and cutting-edge technologies, and impeccable execution of projects layered with an unwavering belief in total design.

Mr. Muntasirul islam, Chief Executive of Spiinfra Constructions, leading the company to make it dynamic and sensitive to the values of Construction industry. With his 24 years of vast varied experience, right from designing, planning, supervision, to procurements, to execution, he is involved in all the activities, till delivery of the finished end product. He had excelled in management of projects to his professional credit, to name a few, like Everest Organics, Neauland Laboratories, NATCO, Eytan Labs, etc., His approach to each and every aspect of the construction process, is to look at the processes from the prospect of Client and Vendor view point, in order to achieve the required outputs by pumping in most insightful inputs, which lead him to the present rising status as Proprietor of Spinfra Constructions. He instilled confidence and discretion in his associative staff, which led the company as successful firm in the construction industry.
